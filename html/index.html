History of tea

China is the home of tea, which is said to have started in the Shennong era, with a history of at least 4700 years. The tea sutra of Lu Yu, the tea sage of the Tang Dynasty, has had a profound influence on tea culture throughout history. Since then, the spirit of tea has permeated the court and society, penetrating into Chinese poetry, painting, calligraphy, religion and medicine. The following video can be used to enter a more in-depth understanding.

The original intention of Time Tea House is not just to sell tea beverages alone, neither is it a service as people understand it. As a tea space it should be a culture, a traditional Chinese culture, such as the store's guqin, calligraphy and painting, bonsai, and porcelain, so that customers have an immersive experience. The moment you send tea into your mouth, Time Tea House allows you to isolate yourself from the hustle and bustle of the world and enter a unique oriental realm. In the end, welcome you to gather here with your family and friends to feel at ease!
